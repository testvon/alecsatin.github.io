Welcome to Comfort for Christians.

This is the personal website of Alec Satin. The site has existed in various forms since 2002. You'll notice fewer images than in the past. This is intentional. The focus is on the words.

Someone once asked Jesus, Who sinned? The man or his parents?

Jesus answered, Neither this man sinned, nor his parents: but that the works of God should be made manifest in him. (John 9:2-3)

No matter what you grapple with in your life, God's grace and goodness can be made manifest in you. This is his will for you and for all his children.

May the things you find here be a help and encouragement to you now and in the future. -Alec Satin
